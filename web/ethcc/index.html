What's up with Habitat?

Scaling DAOs with an Optimistic Rollup

DAOs on Ethereum struggle with low participation rates, plutocracy, and siloed tooling. The main reasons are high gas costs on the Ethereum Mainnet - not only resulting in less voting turnout but also in missing features for incentives or new organisation solutions.

Custom and Crowdfunded Modules

Rollups bring a new and extended design space for DAOs and communities. Thus allowing many different or even complex modules with high throughput. Every organisation can use existing solutions or upvote highly demanded solutions that can be implemented and used by everyone.
